# Prototype Deployment Roadmap: Essential Features

To transition Offrion from a functional API to a demo-ready prototype for merchants and partners, the following features are required to close the business loop and demonstrate value.

---

## 1. Merchant Redemption Terminal (UI)
**The missing bridge between the customer and the merchant.**

- **Concept**: A simplified, mobile-first interface for merchant staff to scan a customer's QR code or enter a 6-digit transaction ID.
- **Workflow**: 
  1. Staff logs in to the Merchant Portal.
  2. Clicks "Scan & Redeem."
  3. Validates the deal and marks it as "Completed" in the database.
- **Impact**: Provides tangible proof of a successful referral and triggers payment flows.

---

## 2. Automated Commission Engine (Logic)
**Ensuring everyone gets paid accurately and instantly.**

- **Concept**: Updating the `/api/merchant/redeem` route to trigger the [Commission](file:///Users/raziquemk/Desktop/OffrionApp/src/models/Commission.ts#3-15) model generation.
- **Logic**:
  - **Partner Share (70%)**: Automatically calculated and credited to the partner's pending balance.
  - **Platform Share (30%)**: Tracked as Offrion's revenue.
  - **Status Management**: Transitions from `pending` (impulse scan) to `confirmed` (merchant redemption).

---

## 3. Visual Performance Dashboards (Analytics)
**Moving beyond numbers to storytelling through data.**

- **Concept**: Integrating a charting library (like `Recharts`) to visualize traffic patterns.
- **Visuals**:
  - **Partner**: Weekly earnings growth and top-performing deal categories.
  - **Merchant**: Daily footfall generated by Offrion and most active partner apps.
- **Impact**: Demonstrates at a glance that the platform is actually working.

---

## 4. Wallet & Payout Management (Fintech)
**The core incentive for partners to stay active.**

- **Concept**: A simple banking tab in the partner/merchant dashboards.
- **Features**:
  - **Balance Overview**: Total Earned, Pending (in-review), and Withdrawable funds.
  - **Transaction Ledger**: Historical list of payouts and fees.
  - **Mock Stripe Integration**: A conceptual "Link Bank Account" flow for the MVP.

---

## 5. Admin Moderation & Quality Control
**Protecting the ecosystem from fraud and low-quality content.**

- **Concept**: A high-level view for platform operators.
- **Features**:
  - **Deal Approval Queue**: Review and approve deals before they hit the public API.
  - **Merchant Verification**: Basic KYC status tracking.
  - **Emergency Kill-switch**: Ability to deactivate fraudulent API keys or deals instantly.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Recommended First Step**: Implementing the **Merchant QR Scanner & Automated Commissions** provides the most critical "Wow Factor" for a prototype demo, as it shows the entire end-to-end flow from API call to financial settlement.
